Getting Started

The Recruiting Squad is a boutique RPO firm that offers a smart, low-cost alternative to contract recruiters and traditional staffing firms. It is staffed by a team of recruiting professionals who manage the full-lifecycle recruiting efforts for your company and you only pay for the hours you need.

The Recruiting Squad differs from traditional RPO Firms in that you only pay for the hours you need, meaning there are no long-term contracts and no agency fees. It is also more cost-effective and time efficient, with a team of seasoned recruiting professionals who use the latest recruiting tools and techniques to fill open positions quickly and effectively.

The Recruiting Squad offers Direct Hire, Contract (Payroll services available), and Contract to Perm (Payroll services available) position types, with work performed for the hours you need.. Services include full lifecycle recruiting, managing job postings, sourcing candidates, screening applicants, conducting interviews, providing reference and background checks, negotiating offers, and managing all paperwork.

The recruiting Squad also offers a Cost Per Hire program. With this program, one of our recruiting managers will work closely with you to develop a recruiting and sourcing strategy that's tailored to your unique needs. Learn more.

The Recruiting Squad charges for our services on a pay-as-you-go basis, where you purchase a block of recruiting hours for your open position(s). You'll only pay for the hours you need, and there are no hidden charges or cancellation fees if you need to start or stop at any time.
