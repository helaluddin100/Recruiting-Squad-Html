How it works.

  • 1. Create Account
  • 2. Schedule Kickoff
  • 3. Purchase Hours
  • 4. Review & Hire
1

Create an Account

Create an account, add your position(s) to our job funnel and get an instant "Cost Estimate" to fill your open positions.

2

Schedule a Kickoff Meeting

Schedule a kickoff meeting with your dedicated Recruiting Manager to discuss your company culture, position(s). and we will develop an advertising and sourcing strategy.

3

Purchase Work Hours

Purchase work hours and post your position(s) to start recruiting. Our recruiters will source candidate resumes and screen applicants who apply to your jobs. We guarantee your cost will never exceed our estimate.

4

Review Resumes and Hire the Best

Review pre-screened candidate resumes in your user-friendly dashboard. Hire as many of the submitted candidates you want at no extra cost.
